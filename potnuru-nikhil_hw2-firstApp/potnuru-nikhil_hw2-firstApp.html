What is Cricket?

Cricket is a popular bat-and-ball sport that is played in many countries around the world, with a particularly strong following in nations like India, England, Australia, Pakistan, and South Africa. It is a team sport that can be played in various formats, but the most common versions of cricket include Test cricket, One-Day International (ODI) cricket, and Twenty20 (T20) cricket. Cricket is known for its rich traditions, with events like Test matches often lasting for several days and featuring breaks for lunch and tea. It has a significant following and is played at both the professional and amateur levels. The sport has also evolved with the introduction of shorter formats like T20, which has led to increased popularity and commercialization.
